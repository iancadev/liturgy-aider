# Waypoints

Always centered text with + (italicized).

These can appear separated sometimes (like PotF + O + LotE -> PotF + O; LotE)

 - Homily + Profession of Faith
 - Prayer of the Faithful + Offertory + Liturgy of the Eucharist
 - Sharing of Peace
 - Prayer to Saint Michael the Archangel
 - Our Father
 - special events (Presentation of Candidates and Sacrament of Confirmation...)











# Events (with information)

These are all one line, centered. No special formatting (except for translation/transcription text supplements)

For special selections (e.g. Renewal of Baptismal Promises), Offertory, Communion

Postlude and Prelude, too

- (Choir|Offertory|Solo:) Title + Composer + Date subtitle

Can include translation/transcription text supplement (narrow text, or light spaced text)
